Your Questions, Answered

Everything You Need to Know

We want your mehndi experience to feel as effortless as it is memorable.

How long does mehndi usually last?
With proper aftercare, a natural henna stain typically lasts 7–14 days. It is darkest during the first 3–5 days after application.
What aftercare tips should I follow?
Keep the paste on for 6–8 hours, avoid water for the first 12 hours and apply a natural balm or oil to protect the stain.
How does the booking process work?
Share your event details through our form or WhatsApp. We confirm availability, discuss your design, and reserve your date with a booking advance.
Do you provide home service?
Yes, we offer home visits across Delhi NCR. Travel charges may apply depending on the location and event timing.
How is pricing decided?
Pricing depends on design intricacy, coverage, number of people and venue. Our packages are a helpful starting guide; request a custom quote anytime.
